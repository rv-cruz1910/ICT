MY POKEMON TEAM

Pokemon Description

Greninja


It can appear and disappear like a ninja.
GRENINJA can fool its opponents with its swift movements.
It can create throwing stars, or "shurikens", with the sharpest of edges to catch and a high speed to catch his opponents off-guard.


Moves
-Dark Pulse
-Hydro Pump
-Ice Beam
-U-turn

Lucario


LUCARIO can feel and understand human emotions. It can do this because it possesses the unique ablity of percieving peoples auras.
Not only does it have the significant ability of reading auras, it also has the ability to manipulate them.
It is said that no prey of LUCARIO can ever escape from it, as it can sense their auras.

Moves
-Swords Dance
-Meteor Mash
-Close Combat
-Bullet Punch

Gengar


Occasionally, on a dark night, your shadow will be randomly thrown by a streetlight, and it will shockingly overtake you. Some say it is actually a GENGAR running past you, posing as your shadow.
To take the life of its victim, it sneaks into its preys shadow, waiting for a moment of vulnerability.

Moves
-Shadow Ball
-Sludge Wave
-Icy Wind / Focus Blast
-Thunder / Taunt / Substitute

Blaziken


BLAZIKEN has legs of great strength— in one leap, it can go through an entire 30-story building. This Pokémon’s scorching punches sets its foes ablaze.
In battle, BLAZIKEN emits intense flames out of its wrists and bravely attacks its enemies. The stronger his opponent is, the more its wrist burns.

Moves
-Swords Dance
-Flare Blitz
-Low Kick
-Stone Edge / Knock Off

Metagross


METAGROSS has four brains in total. Using these 4 brains, it can calculate through things in a breeze, its brain acts even faster than a supercomputer. METAGROSS can tuck in its 4 legs and use it to float.
It possesses not only psychic powers, but also incredibly powerful strength. It grabs prey with its legs and traps them with its claws.

Moves
-Meteor Mash
-Ice Punch
-Rock Slide / Thunder Punch
-Bullet Punch / Earthquake

Tyranitar


A TYRANITAR is very powerful, it can bring down a whole mountain to make its habitat. It roams the mountains to find opponents to battle. Since it has a very strong body, it is very eager to find formidable opponents.
If it finds an opponent that isn't worthy, TYRANITAR ignores it and keeps roaming.

Moves
-Stealth Rock
-Stone Edge
-Pursuit
-Fire Punch / Earthquake
